Milanesa

Indulge in the Argentine culinary tradition with Milanesa, a beloved dish that elevates the simple charm of breaded and fried meat. In this iconic recipe, thin slices of beef or chicken are delicately coated in breadcrumbs, creating a crispy golden exterior that gives way to tender, flavorful goodness. Served with a wedge of lemon, Milanesa is a classic Argentine comfort food, satisfying both the palate and the soul with its delicious simplicity. Get ready to savor a taste of Argentina's culinary heritage in every delightful bite.

Ingredients

  • Beef or chicken
  • Eggs
  • Salt and pepper
  • Breadcrumbs

Steps

  1. Thin slice the meat
  2. Season the breadcrumbs with salt and pepper
  3. Marinate the meat with the eggs
  4. One slice at the time, cover the meet with the breadcrumbs
  5. Fry the milanesas or cook them in the oven
