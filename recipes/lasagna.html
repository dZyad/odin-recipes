Lasagna

Homemade lasagna

Prepare to be captivated, enchanted, and ultimately satiated as you dive into the world of lasagna creation. This recipe is not just a meal; it's a sensory journey, a celebration of taste that transcends the ordinary and elevates your dining experience to extraordinary heights.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground Beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Vegetables
  • Ricotta cheese

Steps

  1. Prepare and cook vegetables for the stuffing and let it rest
  2. Cook sauce with ground beef and let it cool
  3. When sauce is cold mount the lasagna alternating this way sauce -> noodles -> ricotta -> vegetable stuffing -> noodles
  4. Cook in oven
